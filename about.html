About Us

About Us

Raah Education is a premier education consultancy based in Noida Sec 90, with a legacy of over 16 years in guiding students toward their academic goals. We provide comprehensive support and reliable information for admissions across a wide range of disciplines, including:

  • Engineering (B.Tech, M.Tech)
  • Management (BBA, MBA)
  • Law (BA.LLB, BBA.LLB)
  • Commerce (B.Com)
  • Journalism & Mass Communication (BJMC)
  • Computer Applications (BCA, MCA)
  • Medical Studies (MBBS, MD)
  • and other professional courses, both in India and abroad

At Raah Education, we understand the complexities and challenges of the admission process. With increasing competition for spots in top institutions, we provide tailored guidance to help students enhance their chances of securing a place in their desired programs. Our dedicated team of expert consultants works closely with each student, offering personalized advice on selecting the best college, course, or university.
